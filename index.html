How To Use Healing Crystals
Simple ways to incorporate crystals into your daily life
🧘
Meditation
Hold your crystal in your non-dominant hand during meditation. Set an intention and breathe deeply, allowing the crystal's energy to align with yours.
💎
Wear as Jewelry
Wearing crystals as necklaces, bracelets, or rings keeps their energy in your auric field throughout the day for continuous benefit.
🏠
Place in Your Space
Position crystals in key areas of your home — amethyst in the bedroom for calm, citrine in the office for abundance, black tourmaline at the entrance for protection.
🌙
Sleep with Crystals
Place a crystal under your pillow or on your nightstand. Amethyst and selenite are ideal for promoting restful sleep and vivid, meaningful dreams.
💧
Crystal Water
Place crystals around (not in) a glass of water and leave in moonlight overnight. Only use non-toxic crystals like clear quartz or rose quartz.
🌟
Reiki & Energy Work
Place crystals on chakra points during Reiki sessions to amplify healing energy. A certified Reiki Master can guide you in crystal placement for maximum benefit.
🌿
Cleansing Crystals
Cleanse your crystals monthly by placing them in moonlight, smudging with sage, burying briefly in earth, or using sound healing with a singing bowl.
📿
Crystal Grids
Arrange multiple crystals in sacred geometric patterns to amplify their collective energy for specific intentions like love, abundance, or protection.
